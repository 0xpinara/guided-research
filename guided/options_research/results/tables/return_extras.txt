-- VRP gates, VIX level does NOT: evidence AGAINST a dispersion artifact --
If 'high-VRP days are merely high-dispersion days where anything ranks more
easily', then VIX LEVEL (the canonical dispersion proxy) would gate the signal
too. It does not (VIX diffs n.s., p>0.8), while VRP does. The conditioner that
gates is the options market's PRICE of risk, not raw volatility -- so the
high-VRP positive is not a mechanical wider-spread-easier-ranking effect.
